Europe visas

POLAND

Poland is a popular destination for international students, with its high-quality education system and affordable tuition fees. To study in Poland as an international student, you will need to obtain a student visa. You can apply for a Poland student visa at your local Polish embassy or consulate. You will need to submit several documents, including an acceptance letter from a Polish university, proof of financial means to cover your living expenses, and a valid passport.

PORTUGAL

Portugal offers two types of student visas - short-term and long-term visas. Short-term visas are for students who will be in the country for less than 90 days, while long-term visas are for those who plan to stay in Portugal for more than 90 days. To apply for a student visa, students must first obtain an acceptance letter from a recognized educational institution in Portugal. Once they have the letter, they can apply for a visa at the nearest Portuguese consulate or embassy in their home country.

MALTA

Malta is a popular destination for international students seeking higher education. If you're interested in studying in Malta, you'll need to apply for a student visa. To apply for a Malta student visa, you'll need to provide proof of acceptance into a recognized educational institution in Malta, a valid passport, proof of financial support, and proof of health insurance.

CROATIA

Croatia is an increasingly popular destination for international students who want to study in Europe. To study in Croatia as a foreign student, you will need to obtain a student visa. To be eligible for a student visa in Croatia, you will need to first apply and be accepted into a Croatian educational institution. Once you receive an acceptance letter, you can apply for a student visa at the Croatian embassy or consulate in your home country.

ROMANIA

It's important to note that the visa application process can take several weeks or even months, so it's important to start the process early. Once you arrive in Romania, you'll need to register with the local police within 90 days of your arrival. Obtaining a student visa for Romania requires careful planning and attention to detail, but with the right preparation, it can be a straightforward process.

BELARUS

Belarus is a popular destination for international students seeking quality education and cultural experiences.Once you receive an acceptance letter from the university, you can begin the visa application process. Contact the nearest Belarusian embassy or consulate to obtain a list of required documents, which typically include a passport, a completed visa application form, a recent photograph, proof of financial support, and proof of medical insurance.
